TimeLine

Life9 System's
Story so far

•
2016

Beginnings

Company got registered in the month of January. Project kicked off in mid year with a new office and new team members.

•
2018

Milestone 2

  • Built very first IoT platform.
  • First IoT hardware designed and developed for PoC integrated to out IoT platform.
  • Successfully demonstrated a use-case on Industrial IoT for a T1 apparel factory for worker job efficiency monitoring.
•
2020

Milestone 3

Designed, developed and sold, world class end-to-end IoT based smart hand sanitizer during COVID-19 pandemic. Filed 4 patents for the design and features of the product.

•
2021

Milestone 4

  • Moved to a new and better office space.
  • Designed and developed IoT based Smart BulkCooler for all the farmers of FarmConnect for its dairy business.
  • Bagged Indian Defence project from Centre of Artificial Intelligence & Robotics (CAIR) to work on AI based Marine Intelligence for Indian Navy.
•
2022

Milestone 5

Designed and developed an IoT hardware for EV Battery manufacturer and hosted their devices on Life9 Systems IoT platform.

•
2023

Milestone 6

  • Successfully delivered AI based Marine Intelligence project to Indian Defence Organisation (Navy) through CAIR.
  • Initiated a project for FarmConnect (Dairy Company) to work on monitoring cow happiness index for all its farmers cows health assessment.
